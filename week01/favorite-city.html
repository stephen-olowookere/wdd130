Introducing My City

Ogbomoso is a vibrant and historic city located in Oyo State, southwestern Nigeria. It stands as one of the major Yoruba cities, celebrated for its rich cultural heritage and traditional values. The city has a proud history of bravery, unity, and resilience dating back to pre-colonial times.

More About My City

Agriculture forms a vital part of Ogbomosos economy, with crops like yam, maize, and cassava widely grown, and the city also serves as a commercial hub connecting several nearby towns and regions. It is home to the prestigious Ladoke Akintola University of Technology (LAUTECH)
